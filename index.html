Biography Introduction

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo, is a football icon whose name reverberates throughout the world of sports. Born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, Ronaldo's journey from humble beginnings to global superstardom is nothing short of inspirational.
